Birthday Game #1
控制角色避開黑色牆壁，每五面牆就加速一次，獲得10分則可往下一關！
電腦版："方向鍵"上下或"滑鼠點擊"角色上方下方可移動。手機板：觸控角色上方或下方就可移動
Control the character to avoid the black wall. The character will speed up every 5 walls. Get 10 points to go to the next level.
PC: Use "Up Arrow" or "Down Arrow" on keyboard to move the character, Or "Mouse click" above or below the character to move up or down
Moblie: "Touch" above or below the character to move up or down
