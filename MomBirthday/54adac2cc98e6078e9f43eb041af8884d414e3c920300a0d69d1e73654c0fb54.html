Birthday Game #2
點擊往前射一箭，沒有撞到其他箭算一分，每獲得20分(手機10分)則通關！
電腦版："空白鍵"或"滑鼠點擊"可射一箭。手機板：觸控就射一箭
Click to shoot an arrow forward
If you didn't hit another arrow, it will count as a point
Get 20 points(10 points on mobile) to pass the level.
PC: Use "Space" on the keyboard to shoot an arrow, Or use "Mouse click" will shoot an arrow too
Moblie: "Touch" to shoot an arrow.
